Gravy

Gravy is a sauce, often made from the juices of meats that run naturally during cooking and often thickened with wheat flour or corn starch for added texture.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • butter
  • black pepper

Instructions

  1. Drain Pan Drippings
  2. Skim Fat from Drippings
  3. Make a Roux in the Roasting Pan
  4. Add the Reserved Pan Drippings
